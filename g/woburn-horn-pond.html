Ghosts of Massachusetts

Horn Pond

Woburn, MA

Overview

There are multiple sources of hauntings at Horn Pond. These vary from lights that float above the water, a mysterious canoe, and locations where people report their moods change or dogs start barking.

These reports have been linked back to Native American tribes and the legend of the Swan of Light.
