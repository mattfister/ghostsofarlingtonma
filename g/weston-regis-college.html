Ghosts of Massachusetts

Regis College

Weston, MA

Overview

Regis College was founded as a women's college in 1927 and became co-ed in 2007.

Multiple different hauntings have been reported at Regis college. These include a various cold spots, piano music that plays when no one is around, doors that mysteriously open or close, and nuns that haunt the grand foyer.

Room 347 of college Hall has some specific reports of a projector that shut off and on again and a door that slammed shut.
